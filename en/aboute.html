About Trip Rinjani

About Rinjani Trip

Welcome to Rinjani Trip, your trusted partner for an extraordinary trekking experience on Mount Rinjani. Our mission is to provide adventurers with a seamless and memorable journey, exploring one of Indonesia’s most iconic volcanoes.

Who We Are

Rinjani Trip is a premier trekking service provider, deeply integrated with multiple renowned trekking organizers. Our commitment to quality and safety ensures that every trekker embarks on their journey with confidence and peace of mind. We take pride in our collaborative approach, working closely with Taman Nasional Gunung Rinjani to ensure our activities respect and preserve the natural beauty of the region.

Where We’re Located

Our headquarters are located in the picturesque village of Senaru, at Jalan Pariwisata, Bintang Senaru. This prime location not only offers stunning views of Rinjani but also serves as the perfect starting point for your adventure.

Our Commitment

At Rinjani Trip, we are committed to delivering an unparalleled trekking experience that aligns with sustainable tourism practices. Our collaboration with Taman Nasional Gunung Rinjani reflects our dedication to preserving the environment while providing adventurers with an authentic and responsible way to explore Mount Rinjani.

Join Us

Whether you’re an experienced trekker or new to the world of hiking, Rinjani Trip is here to guide you through every step of your journey. We invite you to join us in discovering the awe-inspiring landscapes and challenging trails that make Mount Rinjani a bucket-list destination for adventurers around the globe.
