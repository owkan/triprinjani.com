Hiking Rinjani and Rafting

140$ / pax

Conquer Mount Rinjani and ride the rapid - 4 day 3 night

Join us for an unforgettable journey through Lombok's natural wonders. Book your adventure today and make memories that will last forever!

Highlight

  • Sunrise at the summit
  • Stunning views of Segara Anak Lake
  • Natural hot springs
  • Exhilarating rapids
  • Crystal-clear waters

Included

  • Profesional mountain guide and Porters
  • Meals during the hike
  • Camping equipment
  • Rinjani park entry ticket and insurance
  • 1 night accomodation in Senaru
  • Professional rafting guides
  • Rafting Safety gear
  • welcome drink and snacks at Rafting basecamp
  • lunch
  • Transport pick up and drop off

Itinerary

Day 1 (Rafting)

  • pick up at hotel or somewhere in lombok around 8am
  • Rafting actifity
  • Lunch at Rafting basecamp
  • Transfer to accomodation in Senaru village
  • Walk around the village and waterfall before sunset
  • Breafing and detail hiking trip around 7pm at our office or restauran.

Day 2 (Hiking)

  • Prepare and breakfast at hotel
  • transfer to Rinjani office at sembalun for registration.
  • Start hiking
  • lunch at pos 2
  • dinner and over night at Crater rim Sembalun

Day 3 (Hiking)

  • wake up and have a light breakfast and hot drink at 1.30am then climb up to the summit.
  • descend to the lake, soaking in the hot spring and lunch
  • climb up to crater rim Senaru, dinner and over night

Day 4 (Finish the hike)

  • Breakfast while enjoy the best view of entire crater lake and volcano
  • Taking photos
  • descend to Senaru village
  • Having lunch at pos 1 or at the end of the trail/National park gate
  • Conclud the hike
  • transfer to the next destition

Note:

  • Every aspiring hiker to Mount Rinjani is required to make reservations well in advance to avoid running out of tickets.
  • bring a jacket or warm clothes suitable for mountain weather.
  • bring a flashlight because you will be hiking to the summit early in the morning before sunrise.
  • wear shoes suitable for hiking.
  • prepare extra food and drinks in your personal bag as supplies for the journey before the team provides meals.
  • If you don't have shoes, a jacket, or a flashlight, you can rent them from the rental services around our place.
