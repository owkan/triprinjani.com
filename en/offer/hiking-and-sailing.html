140$ / pax

Rinjani Trekking & Komodo Sailing Adventure

This combined trekking and sailing package offers the best of both worlds: the thrill of a challenging hike and the relaxation of a scenic sailing adventure. Perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

ITINERARY Rinjani 3d 2n

Day 1 (Registration-Start Hiking- Sembalun Crater rim)

  • Prepare and breakfast at hotel
  • transfer to Rinjani office at sembalun for registration.
  • Start hiking
  • lunch at pos 2
  • dinner and over night at Crater rim Sembalun

Day 2 (Summit-Lake-Hot spring)

  • wake up and have a light breakfast and hot drink at 1.30am then climb up to the summit.
  • descend to the lake, soaking in the hot spring and lunch
  • climb up to crater rim Senaru, dinner and over night

Day 3 (Crater rim-Senaru village)

  • Breakfast while enjoy the best view of entire crater lake and volcano
  • Taking photos
  • descend to Senaru village
  • Having lunch at pos 1 or at the end of the trail/National park gate
  • finish hiking
  • transfer to the next destination in Lombok

Included

  • Transport pick up and drop off.
  • Accomodation/hotel bevore hike
  • e-Rinjani Ticket
  • Insurance
  • Guide
  • Poerter
  • 3 x meals during the hike
  • Mattress
  • 1 Tend for 2 people
  • Sleeping bag

Things are not included and are important to bring.

  • Torch/headlamp
  • Trekking pole
  • Jacket
  • Shoes and other personal stuff.
  • Extra personal food and drinks.

Komodo Sailing 4d 3n

  • Lombok
  • | Sumbawa
  • | Komodo island
  • | Flores

ITINERARY

Day 1: Bangsal Harbour (Dicky Cafe) Labuhan Lombok Kenawa Island

  • 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM Meet at Dicky Cafe.
  • 11:00 AM: Depart for Kayangan Harbour after a travel briefing.
  • Start the sail

Day 2: Saleh Bay Sumbawa (Whale Shark Point)

  • Swim with whale sharks.
  • Continue cruising to Komodo Island with a quick swim stop at one of the beaches on Sumbawa Island.

Day 3: Komodo Island Long Pink Beach Padar Island

  • Explore Komodo Island and see the dragons in their natural habitat.
  • Relax, snorkel, and enjoy beach games at Long Pink Beach.
  • Soft trekking to watch the sunset from the top of Padar Hill.

Day 4: Manjarite Point Kelor Island Labuan Bajo (Flores)

  • Snorkeling and swimming at Manjarite Point.
  • Snorkeling and hiking at Kelor Island.

Included

  • 4 Days, 3 Nights Boat Trip
  • Meals During the Trip
  • Unlimited Drinking Water, Coffee, and Tea
  • Entrance Fee to Komodo National Park
  • English Speaking Tour Guide
  • Snorkeling Equipment (Mask and Snorkel)

Excluded

  • Hotel in Lombok or Labuan Bajo
  • Alcoholic Drinks, Soft Drinks, and Snacks
  • Travel Insurance
  • Transfer to Your Hotel or Airport in Labuan Bajo
  • Towels
  • Personal Expenses

Complementaries

  • Free pick-up from accommodation in Senggigi or Mataram.

Note:

  • Departure every monday and Thursday
  • Pick-up outside the Senggigi and Mataram areas is available at an extra charge
  • Bring your towel, swimsuits, sunscreen, trekking shoes, snacks, and extra drinks.
