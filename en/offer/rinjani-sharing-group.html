Rinjani Sharing Group

130$ / pax

Create your own group, Hike Together, Save Together

Are you ready for an unforgettable adventure? Mount Rinjani, the second highest volcano in Indonesia, offers breathtaking views, challenging trails, and the thrill of conquering a majestic peak. Now, we have an exciting offer that makes this experience even better!

Here's the deal:

Gather your friends and create your own group of at least six people to hike mount Rinjani together, you’ll enjoy special discounts and benefits that make this adventure more affordable and fun.

Here’s what you’ll get:

  • Discounted Rates: Save more when you hike as a group. The more friends you bring, the more you save!
  • Guide and Porters Services: Our experienced Guide and Porters will ensure your group has a safe and enjoyable trek.
  • Comprehensive pre-hike briefing and preparation tips.
  • All Rinjani necessary permits and fees ( Ticket and insurance)
  • Meals, snacks, and hydration during the hike.
  • Camping equipment and accommodation.
  • Group Activities: Participate in special group activities and bonding sessions to make your trek even more memorable

How to Get Started and Join ?

  1. Gather your friends and creat a group of at least 6 people.
  2. Contact us to book your preferred dates.
  3. Get ready for an epic adventure!

ITINERARY

Day 1 (Registration-Start Hiking- Sembalun Crater rim)

  • Prepare and breakfast at hotel
  • transfer to Rinjani office at sembalun for registration.
  • Start hiking
  • lunch at pos 2
  • dinner and over night at Crater rim Sembalun

Day 2 (Summit-Lake-Hot spring)

  • wake up and have a light breakfast and hot drink at 1.30am then climb up to the summit.
  • descend to the lake, soaking in the hot spring and lunch
  • climb up to crater rim Senaru, dinner and over night

Day 3 (Crater rim-Senaru village)

  • Breakfast while enjoy the best view of entire crater lake and volcano
  • Taking photos
  • descend to Senaru village
  • Having lunch at pos 1 or at the end of the trail/National park gate
  • finish hiking
  • transfer to the next destination in Lombok

Note:

  • Every aspiring hiker to Mount Rinjani is required to make reservations well in advance to avoid running out of tickets.
  • bring a jacket or warm clothes suitable for mountain weather.
  • bring a flashlight because you will be hiking to the summit early in the morning before sunrise.
  • wear shoes suitable for hiking.
  • prepare extra food and drinks in your personal bag as supplies for the journey before the team provides meals.
  • If you don't have shoes, a jacket, or a flashlight, you can rent them from the rental services around our place.
