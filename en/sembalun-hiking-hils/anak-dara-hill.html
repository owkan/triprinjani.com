Hiking Anak Dara Hill

Anak Dara Hill, also known as Maiden Hill, is a stunning peak in the Sembalun Seven Summits, located east of Mount Rinjani. Standing at 1,923 meters above sea level, it offers breathtaking views and is slightly higher than its neighbor, Pergasingan Hill. Located in East Lombok's Sembalun Village, this hill is a must-visit for local and international tourists.

Hike Details

Reaching the summit of Anak Dara Hill from Sembalun Village takes about 3 hours, including hiking, trekking, and some light climbing. The descent back to the village takes around 2 hours. The exact duration depends on your pace and fitness level. This trip is ideal for those with limited time or who are not ready to tackle the more challenging Mount Rinjani hike.
