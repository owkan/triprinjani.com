Great! Trekking to the Crater Rim Senaru of Rinjani is an exciting adventure for any nature lover or outdoor enthusiast. Here's an itinerary for a 2-day, 1-night trek to the Crater Rim Senaru of Rinjani:

Included

  • e-Rinjani Ticket
  • Insurance
  • Guide
  • Poerter
  • Ojek/transfer to starting point
  • Snack
  • 3 x meals during the hike
  • Tend
  • Mattress
  • Sleeping bag

Things are not included and are important to bring.

  • Accomodation/hotel
  • Trekking pole
  • Jacket
  • Shoes and other personal stuff.
  • Transport pick up and drop off.

ITINERARY

Day 1: Aik Berik Village - Kondo Camp

In the morning, after breakfast at the homestay, you'll be transported by motorbike to the Mount Rinjani National Park boundary, a journey of about 30 minutes. From there, you'll begin your hike towards Pos 1 and Pos 2, where lunch will be served. After a rest and lunch, you'll continue your journey to the campsite, where dinner will be provided and you will spend the night.

Day 2: 2nd Summit - Campsite - Aik Berik Village

Wake up early around 3 AM to prepare for the hike to Mount Rinjani's second highest summit, where you can enjoy the stunning views of the mountain against the backdrop of the sunrise. Afterward, you'll descend back to the campsite for breakfast and prepare for the descent back to Aik Berik Village. Lunch will be served at Pos 2. Then, you'll continue your descent to the national park boundary, where motorbikes will be waiting to take you back to Aik Berik Village.

Note:

  • Every aspiring hiker to Mount Rinjani is required to make reservations well in advance to avoid running out of tickets.
  • bring a jacket or warm clothes suitable for mountain weather.
  • wear shoes suitable for hiking.
  • If you don't have shoes, a jacket, you can rent them from the rental services around our place.
