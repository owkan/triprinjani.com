Great! Trekking to the Crater Rim Senaru of Rinjani is an exciting adventure for any nature lover or outdoor enthusiast. Here's an itinerary for a 2-day, 1-night trek to the Crater Rim Senaru of Rinjani:

Pre-Hike Stay

You are required to stay in Senaru for 1 night before the hiking day to prepare and receive detailed explanations about the Rinjani trek from our team.

Included

  • e-Rinjani Ticket
  • Insurance
  • Guide
  • Poerter
  • 3 x meals during the hike
  • 1 Tend for 2 people
  • Mattress
  • Sleeping bag

Things are not included and are important to bring.

  • Torch/headlamp
  • Trekking pole
  • Jacket
  • Shoes and other personal stuff.
  • Extra personal food and drinks.
  • Accomodation/hotel
  • Transport pick up and drop off.

ITINERARY

Day 1

  • Transfer to the Rinjani Trek Centre at Senaru village (1,150m) where you will register for the trek
  • Start trekking to the POS 1 (1,300m) and then POS 2 (1,500m) where you will have a break for some snacks and water.
  • Continue to POS 3 (1,800m) and stop for lunch and rest.
  • After lunch, continue trekking to the Senaru Crater Rim (2,641m), where you will arrive in the late afternoon to watch the stunning sunset view over Mount Agung in Bali and the Gili Islands.
  • dinner and over night at Senaru crater rim

Day 2

  • Wake up early in the morning to see the breathtaking sunrise view over the crater and lake with hot Coffee in hand.
  • After breakfast, descend down the Senaru trail to POS 3 and then POS 2, where you will have lunch.
  • Continue trekking down to POS 1 and back to the Rinjani Trek Centre or Senaru village.

Note:

  • Every aspiring hiker to Mount Rinjani is required to make reservations well in advance to avoid running out of tickets.
  • bring a jacket or warm clothes suitable for mountain weather.
  • bring a flashlight because you will be hiking to the summit early in the morning before sunrise.
  • wear shoes suitable for hiking.
  • prepare extra food and drinks in your personal bag as supplies for the journey before the team provides meals.
  • If you don't have shoes, a jacket, or a flashlight, you can rent them from the rental services around our place.
